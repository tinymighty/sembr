sembr

Beautifully simple crop tracking

Sembr is a tool to help farmers and gardeners plan crops and track yields.

An offline-first browser based tracker to help you manage your crops in style and simplicity. We’re gearing up to release an early prototype for your feedback. You can keep an eye on the blog for updates, and if you enter your email address below we’ll give you a shout when we’re ready!
